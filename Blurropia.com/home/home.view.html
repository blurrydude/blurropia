  • status: installed and working
    Pet Shrinking Posts
    Description

    Allows players to conveniently store their pets and call them out without the need to stable them. Pets left beyond the normal stable limits will be lost forever if they are not bonded. Shrunken pets do not take control slots, but unshrunk pets will, so if you have too many pets out, you will not be able to unshrink another.

    Usage

    Double click the hitching post and then target the pet to shrink. You will receive a statuette of your pet. Double click the statuette while it is in your backpack to unshrink it.

Shard Rules

  • The First Law of Blurropia is there will be no hate here. We will not tolerate hate speech of any kind. If you do not agree with or like another player, there is plenty of room in the world and--at the most extreme--there are other servers. Any hate speech will be met by swift banishment from the server.

  • The Second Law of Blurropia is PVP must be consensual and carried out in the appropriate areas. If you happen to guild up, please keep any potential guild PVP away from largely used areas like West Brit Bank. If this becomes a problem, it may result in up to two days in jail with no travel or chat access, and/or a 10,000 gold fine.

  • The Third Law of Blurropia is the Staff are final judge, jury, executioner, and tax collector--what they say ultimately goes so long as it does not break these rules. We're here to have fun and to enjoy the benefits of having multiple gamers together in one community. All grievances will be brought before at least one admin and one witness (admin or player) from outside the dispute. Grievances can be posted on Discord and dealt with in a private text/voice channel, or in game, but whatever medium is agreed upon must be agreed upon and attended by all parties.
